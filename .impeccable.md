## Design Context

### Users
Developers, terminal-heavy engineers, and AI-agent power users evaluating con as a serious daily terminal with an agent harness built into the workflow.

### Brand Personality
Fast, humane, precise. The brand should feel competent and quietly memorable: technical enough for serious engineers, but warmer than a conventional developer-tool landing page.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined cinematic dark interface with restrained warmth, crisp typography, and product-native details. Use the existing con icon, Nowledge Labs mark, night-sky/raccoon hero artwork, and terminal/agent language. Avoid generic neon cyberpunk, busy gradient cards, and template-like SaaS hero layouts.

### Design Principles
- Lead with a clear product signal in the first viewport and in social cards.
- Keep typography strong, spacious, and readable at small preview sizes.
- Use delight through one or two memorable brand details, not decorative clutter.
- Favor tinted dark neutrals, warm off-white text, and subtle green/amber terminal accents.
- Treat social preview images as product artifacts: exact 1200x630 framing, absolute metadata, and crawler-safe static assets.
